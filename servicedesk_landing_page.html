Features

Everything your IT team needs

Smart ticket routing

Live SLA monitoring
Green / yellow / red indicators show SLA health in real time. Breaches trigger automatic manager escalation.

Role-based dashboards
Employees, support agents, admins, and managers each get a tailored view — only the data that matters to them.

Multi-tenant isolation
Each company domain is fully isolated. Bluvium can't see ABCSoft's tickets — ever. Enforced at the data layer.

Email notifications
Ticket created, assigned, updated, SLA warning, resolved — every event triggers the right email to the right person.

Analytics & reports
MTTR, SLA compliance %, technician performance, department trends. Export to PDF or CSV on demand.

RBAC security

Full ticket lifecycle

Threaded comments
